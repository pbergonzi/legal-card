About Us

Attorney Travel Assistance is an organization located in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The Company strives to provide legal services to all foreign visitors along and across Argentina. Our attorney members are committed to providing high quality legal representation and advisory to their clients in a vast variety of matters. We have a wide network of attorneys and paralegals ready to walk you through any legal encounter in Argentina from a Passport theft to a car crash setting. We got you and your family covered.
