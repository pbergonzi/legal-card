FAQ

Why do we need Attorney Assistance? Fast Access to legal aid is something that visitors typically take for granted while at home or in their daily workplace locations, where they are normally familiar with the local legal system and language. This is not the case when visitors are travelling, so Attorney Assistance provides all subscribers with low cost access to legal assistance along and across Argentina.

What is covered? Full details are set out in the Terms & Conditions available on the Attorney Assistance website. In brief, the coverage comprises: legal assistance of any sort for the subscribers ,including spouses and children.

When am I covered? You are fully covered ever since you purchase any of our coverage plans on the web before or upon arrival to Argentina.

Who is covered? Cover is provided to each subscriber and his/her accompanying spouses and children.

Do I need to inform someone about pre-existing legal conditions? As long as you are fit to enter Argentina through Customs, you are covered and do not need to inform anyone

Are there any restrictions on where I am covered? No. Coverage is available all through the territory of Argentina.

Who do I contact in the event of an incident? You can either contact us 24/7 by phone + (5411) 4871-6347, or by email at contact@attorney-assistance.com

What details do I need to give? The helpline number and email is dedicated to all of our subscribers. You should only provide your Name and way of contact. Further information may by be required depending on the matter reported so as to assist you better.

Is assistance available in different languages? Assistance is available in both Spanish and English.
